ABOUT US

“100% organic” is a label used to describe products that contain 100% organic ingredients, excluding salt and water. This label can be used for most raw, unprocessed, or minimally processed farm crops. The Organic Content Standard (OCS 100) is a production standard that verifies the amount of organic material in non-food products. It also tracks the flow of raw materials from their source to the final product. OCS 100 can be used as a business-to-business tool to ensure that companies are selling quality products, as well as many grains, oats, and flours. The OCS applies to any non-food product containing 95-100 percent organic material. It verifies the presence and amount of organic material in a final product and tracks the flow of the raw material from its source to the final product. The OCS 100 relies on third-party verification to confirm whether a final product contains the accurate amount of a given organically grown material. OCS allows for transparent, consistent, and comprehensive independent evaluation and verification of organic material content claims on products. It can be used as a business-to-business tool to give companies the means to ensure that they are selling quality and getting what they pay for.
